Identifying COVID-19

April 19, 2020

A utility to help identify COVID-19 from the flu, common cold, or allergies. Note that this utility assumes that you have some sort of disease, and that you are showing symptoms. It cannot detect if your symptoms have not shown yet you are still a carrier. The utility can only work with the information that you give it. Please note that this utility is by no means accurate and should be taken with a grain of salt. It should not replace professional advice from your physician. If you have any doubts, please get medical attention.
