Latest Project: Identifying COVID-19

April 19, 2020

During this unpredictable time, it can be hard to differentiate common cold from influenza, and influenza from the novel coronavirus, along with many more respiratory related diseases. For this reason, I have created a utility to help you find out if the disease is the common cold, influenza, or the novel coronavirus. Please note that this utility is by no means accurate and should be taken with a grain of salt. It should not replace professional advice from your physician. If you have any doubts, please get medical attention.
